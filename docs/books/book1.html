Dolphins at Gulf World

By:

Hoidberg

We were waiting in the seats in the locker room at gulf world, after a while of waiting it was time for our group to swim with the dolphins, I was so excited to finally swim with the dolphins. we then walked up to the life jackets and put them on. after putting them on we then walked to the seats and waited.

Swimming with the Dolphins

After waiting, it was finally our chance to swim with the dolphins! We walked up to the pool and got in, after a while of waiting in the pool it was finally my turn to do the dolphin thing. after taking the picture I swam to the middle and the dolphin came over, I grabbed the fin of the dolphin and the dolphin swam back to the place where we were sitting and after another while of waiting we were finally done.
